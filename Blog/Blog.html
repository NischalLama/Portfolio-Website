Impact of Technology In My Life

Impact of Technology in the daily life

Due to technology the day to day activites has become so much easier and less time consuming. Nowadays, if i want to check the weather forecast i can easily just open my mobile and check it, if i want to read books i can easily ready books online, if i want to gain knowledge then all i have to do is surf the internet. Things have become so much simpler now.

Negative Impacts

Technology has also affected my life negatively. Before, i used to have a perfect 20/20 vision, but now after overuse of computers and mobiles i require glasses inorder to see properly. Computers and mobiles send rays to our eyes which is very harmful.

Positive Effects

Nowadays, i can easily check my heart rate, how much i ran and how long i worked out with just my phone. You can find alot of these types of apps nowadays very easily.
